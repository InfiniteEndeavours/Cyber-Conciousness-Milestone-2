Who are you? What is this site for?

Well, I'm Ethan. I'm a 21-year-old IT Analyst from Wales. I've been working in the IT sector for just over 3 years now. I currently support a business over around 200 users over different locations. In my role, I am responsible for the security of our business, but also on training our users to be safe in a Cyber world. I'm currently looking to advance my career into the world of Web Development.

I designed this site mainly as a project for the course I am undertaking, but the second reason is that I feel people are not prepared enough for the digital world. You hear all to often about people being scammed out of money, having their precious files deleted, or even having to pay to get access back to their devices.

My goal with this site is to help arm users of the internet with tools and information that can be used to help keep themselves, their families and friends safe online.

Brief:

This site is designed to help people be more secure online. We offer resources and tools to help achieve this.
