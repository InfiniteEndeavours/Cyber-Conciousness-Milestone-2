Helpful Resources

Bitwarden is a free and easy to use password manager. Able to sync between all your devices, the ability to autofill passwords, and receive alerts if your password is detected in a breach! Bitwarden is a great starting password manager.

 A link to Bitwarden, a password manager.

This article from TechSafety has great tips on how to increase the security of your accounts. Some of the main tips are to focus on the length and complexity of a password. A longer password with numbers and symbols is much harder for a hacker to brute force your passwords.

 A link to an article from Tech Safety.

The UK's National Cyber Security Centre recommends using three random words, a passphrase! This article has great suggestions on making a secure password using words. Just remember, to always avoid using dictionary words in your passwords! Substitute letters for numbers or symbols.
